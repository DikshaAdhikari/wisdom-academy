WISDOM ACADEMY

Work is Worship

We are a community of learners

At Wisdom Academy, we aim to provide the highest standard education equipped with best facility and environment for overall development of small children. Children are the wonderful gift of God and initial years are very important to set the right direction, objectives and goals of their lives. Wisdom Academy has successfully completed 25 years of imparting the highest quality of education to over 10,000 children. Over these 25 years, the school has grown many folds in every direction but our model and the core beliefs have remained the same. Our model is based on providing the highest quality yet affordable nursery and elementary education to children of our city. We believe that education is the main tool that will help our children to evolve into accomplished students equipped with strong basic knowledge fundamentals, superior ethical values and self-confidence to profit from their senior and secondary education as well as to evolve into a successful person and a good human being. We also note that this very long and successful journey was only made possible by the belief of the parents in the "Wisdom" brand and their continued support and patronage alongside the vision of the management and the immense efforts and love of the staff. Looking backward we see a job well done but going forward we continue to strive to keep the standards maintained and deliver better and better education.
